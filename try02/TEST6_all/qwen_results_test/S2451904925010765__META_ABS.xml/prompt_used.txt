To regulate the yield of tar and char during biomass pyrolysis, a lattice Boltzmann method that couples heat and mass transfer with chemical reactions is developed for simulating particle pyrolysis and product evolution in this study. The simulation results reveal that circular particle exhibits the highest char yield (25.46 %), while diamond-shaped particle has the highest tar yield (69.95 %). The increase in inlet gas rate leads to an increase in heat per unit time and an uneven temperature, which promotes tar and syngas generation.
