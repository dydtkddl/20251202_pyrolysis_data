Empowering sustainability: Charting the seven years of progress in g-C₃N₄ based materials and their crucial role in building a greener future Graphitic carbon nitride has recently attracted attention due to its unique structural properties and versatile applications in catalysis. This review article gives a comprehensive view of the synthesis methods, structure-property relationships, and diverse applications of g-C₃N₄. Starting with exploring synthesis techniques, including thermal condensation and template-assisted approaches, the review elucidates the influence of precursor materials, reaction conditions, and post-treatment strategies on the morphology and catalytic performance of g-C₃N₄. Furthermore, it investigates the applications of g-C₃N₄ across various domains such as environmental remediation, energy conversion, sensing, and electrocatalysis. By examining the underlying mechanisms, recent advancements, and prospects in each application area, this review underscores the transformative potential of g-C₃N₄ in addressing global challenges and advancing sustainable technologies. Critical analysis of synthesis routes and performance parameters aims to guide the design of innovative solutions, fostering the development of proficient and eco-friendly materials for a sustainable future.
