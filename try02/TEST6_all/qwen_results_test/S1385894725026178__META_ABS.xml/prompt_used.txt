Efficient waste polyester plastics recycling in organic–inorganic acid catalytic system To solve the growing waste plastics problems, we herein reported an efficient method for polyester plastics recycling under mild conditions (<100 °C) by using formic acid (FA) and tungsten-based polyoxometalate (phosphotungstic acid, PW12) as the organic–inorganic catalyst system.
