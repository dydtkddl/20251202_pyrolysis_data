Review on progress of rare earth science and technology in 2024 This article briefly reviews the major research progress of 9 typical material fields, including rare earth luminescence materials, catalysis, rare earth materials for biomedical applications, magnetic materials, optical crystals, molecular-based materials, energy materials, metals and alloys, as well as rare earth extraction, separation and recycling in the year 2024. The aim of the review is to summarize the past and look into the future, and it will provide a basic overview of domestic work in related fields last year. Due to the abundance of content, the review strives to be concise.
