While PVC and PP have been studied separately, the coupled transport and reaction dynamics governing chlorine release during gasification are not well understood. Here we developed a two–dimensional CFD-Dense Discrete Phase Model (DDPM) model to simulate downdraft gasification of a 50:50 MPW blend in a lab–scale reactor of 0.2 m diameter, 0.55 m height.
