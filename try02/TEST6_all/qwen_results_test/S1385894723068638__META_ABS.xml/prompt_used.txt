Herein, we find that diphenyl phosphate (DPP), a cheap organocatalyst, can efficiently catalyze PLA hydrolysis into valuable oligomers/lactic acid. Moreover, DPP can also effectively catalyze the hydrolysis of other polyesters and polycarbonates. Overall, this study is expected to address the environmental and engineering challenges associated with plastic waste disposal, in particular fostering a PLA-based green circular bio-economy.
