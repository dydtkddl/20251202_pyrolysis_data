The use of peat in wastewater treatment Peat has been widely used in the treatment of wastewaters. The potential of using peat in wastewater treatment is reviewed with special attention to the following topics: (i) the properties of peat; (ii) the pretreatment of peat; (iii) the principles involved in the removal of wastewater pollutants by peat; and (iv) the applications of peat to the removal of impurities from wastewater.
