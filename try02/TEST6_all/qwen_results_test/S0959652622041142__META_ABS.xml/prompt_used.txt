In this study, we propose a novel enhancement mechanism based on the enhanced constructive interference for microwave-assisted regeneration of the activated carbons via a facile method for preparing activated carbons. We prepared activated carbons with similar adsorption characteristics (qe ≈ 0.20 g g−1 with a toluene concentration of 376 mg m−3) and different dielectric properties (29.96 95%) and reduced energy consumption (∼50% of maximum value) in AC-450 with an input power of 40 W.
