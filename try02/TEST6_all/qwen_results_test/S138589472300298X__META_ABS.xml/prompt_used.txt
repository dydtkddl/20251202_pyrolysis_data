The experiment was carried out at the melting temperature of 250 ℃, the pyrolysis temperature of 550 ℃, and the catalytic temperature of 450 ℃. The co-feeding of the WEO and molten LDPE effectively promoted the formation of light aromatics, with the content of monocyclic aromatic hydrocarbons as high as 82.69 % (LDPE/WEO = 1:3) and the relative content of BTEX (benzene, toluene, ethylbenzene, xylenes) as high as 65.96 %.
