Leveraging the close chemical bonding between Ni and Mo atoms, the NiMo catalyst can undergo in-situ self-sulfidation to generate active NiMoS sites and a composite structure of Ni3S2 and MoS2 nano phase in tight contact, thus exhibiting strong synergistic effects.
