Microalgae for biofuels: A review of thermochemical conversion processes and associated opportunities and challenges Biofuel production and its research is gaining attention due to ever-increasing concerns over the depletion of fossil fuels and their devastating impact on the environment. This article reviews the various thermochemical conversion processes of biomass, focussing on microalgae. A particular emphasis of this review is the effect of experimental parameters, and feedstock pre-treatment in improving the properties of microalgae derived biofuel via torrefaction, hydrothermal liquefaction, gasification, combustion and pyrolysis processes. This review discusses the prospect and challenges of the thermochemical conversion processes of microalgae.
