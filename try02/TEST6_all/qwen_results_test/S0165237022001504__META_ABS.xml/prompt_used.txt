The pyrolysis of LoS can be divided into three stages. Even II (200–600 ℃) and Even III (600–900 ℃) were responsible for devolatilization. The pyrolysis in Even II and Even III had an activation energy of 192.3 kJ/mol and 178.5 kJ/mol, respectively, with mechanism functions of nth-order reaction f(α) = (1-α)4.74 and contracting cylinder f(α) = 2(1-α)1/2 models, respectively.
