A comprehensive sensitivity analysis is carried out to impart the influence of important process parameters, viz. equivalence ratio(ER: 0.1–0.5), feed plastic content(FPC: 0–30 %), steam to feed ratio(SFR: 0–4) and steam temperature(150–1000 °C) on performance parameters, viz. syngas composition, gasification temperature(GT), lower heating value(LHV), cold gas efficiency(CGE), gasification energy efficiency(GEE) and hydrogen energy efficiency(HEE).
