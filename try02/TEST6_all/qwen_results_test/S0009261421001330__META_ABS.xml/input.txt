Pyrolysis and carbonization of polyvinyl chloride under electric field: A computational study In this article, the thermal decomposition mechanism of polyvinyl chloride (PVC) model compounds under electric field have been investigated by employing density functional theory method in order to explore the effect and mechanism of PVC pyrolysis and carbonization under the action of electric field. By adding electric field into several possible pyrolysis pathways, the thermodynamic and kinetic parameters were calculated.
