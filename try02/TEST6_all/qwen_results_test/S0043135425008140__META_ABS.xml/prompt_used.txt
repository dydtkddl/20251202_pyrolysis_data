This study addresses these limitations through the innovative synthesis of a metal-organic framework (MOF)-derived Fe-Si micro-nucleus (PFMS), engineered to drive a robust anammox system at lower micro-nuclei doses and mitigate potential risks of excessive iron inhibition.
