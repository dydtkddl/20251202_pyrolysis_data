TGA experiments indicated that the pyrolysis of PS proceeded in three phases, and the thermographs shifted to higher temperatures with increasing heating rate. Chars FTIR showed that the absorption of O–H, C–H, CO and C–C decreased with pyrolysis temperatures increasing.
